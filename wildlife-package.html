Welcome to the Jungle!

Sri Lanka is one of the best places in Asia for seeing wildlife. On this tour, you will visit the most famous national parks, each offering a unique experience.

Spot the elusive leopard in Yala, watch elephants bathe in Udawalawe, see sloth bears in Wilpattu, and observe thousands of migratory birds in Kumana. This is the ultimate safari experience.

Travel to Wilpattu, the land of lakes ("Willu"). It is famous for leopards and sloth bears. Enjoy full day safaris.

Attraction

Activity

Elephant Safari

Day 6-7
EAST / AREA 04

Gal Oya National Park

Experience a unique boat safari on the Senanayake Samudra reservoir. Watch elephants swimming between islands.

Attraction

Senanayake Samudra, Gal Oya

Activity

Boat Safari, Jungle Walk with Veddas

Day 8-9
SOUTH EAST / AREA 05

Kumana National Park

Kumana is Sri Lanka's bird watching paradise. It is also home to leopards and elephants, but less crowded than Yala.

Attraction

Kumana Villu (Bird Sanctuary)

Activity

Bird Watching, Safari

Day 10-11
SOUTH / AREA 06

Yala National Park

The most famous park for leopards. Enjoy multiple game drives to increase your chances of spotting the big cats.

Yala National Park

Activity

Tips for your wildlife adventure

Early morning (6:00 AM - 9:00 AM) and late afternoon (3:00 PM - 6:00 PM) are the best times. Yala Block 1 is famous for sightings, but Wilpattu offers a more secluded experience.
Yes, it is very safe. You will be in a sturdy 4x4 jeep with an experienced driver and often a park tracker. Do not get out of the vehicle unless permitted.
A telephoto lens of at least 300mm to 400mm is recommended for birds and elusive animals. A wider lens is great for landscapes.
Most parks are open year-round. However, Yala Block 1 sometimes closes in September/October for drought relief. During this time, we visit other blocks or parks like Bundala.
