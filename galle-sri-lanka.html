Galle Sri Lanka

Where History, Culture, and Coastal Charm Converge on Sri Lanka's Southern Shores

A Historic Coastal Heritage City

Galle is located on Sri Lanka’s southern coast and is best known for its beautifully preserved colonial architecture, oceanfront charm, and vibrant cultural atmosphere. Centered around the iconic Galle Fort, the city blends cobblestone streets, boutique cafés, and seaside views into a calm yet sophisticated travel experience.

Galle Fort is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the finest examples of a fortified city built by European powers in South Asia. Shaped by Portuguese and later Dutch influence, the fort remains a living heritage district where history, local life, and modern coastal culture meet.

Must Visit Attractions

01

Galle Fort

A UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the best-preserved colonial forts in Asia, featuring cobblestone streets, historic ramparts, boutique cafés, and stunning ocean views.

02

Galle Lighthouse

The oldest lighthouse in Sri Lanka, standing proudly at the edge of Galle Fort and offering picturesque coastal views and a peaceful seaside atmosphere.

03

Dutch Reformed Church

A historic 18th-century church within Galle Fort, known for its colonial architecture, antique gravestones, and deep historical significance.

04

National Maritime Museum

A museum dedicated to Sri Lanka’s maritime history, showcasing traditional fishing techniques, shipwreck artifacts, and the island’s long relationship with the sea.

05

Jungle Beach

A secluded beach surrounded by lush greenery, offering calm waters, scenic views, and a tranquil escape just a short drive from Galle Fort.

Things To Do

Explore Galle Fort on Foot

Walk through the UNESCO-listed Galle Fort to discover colonial streets, boutiques, museums, cafés, and scenic ramparts overlooking the Indian Ocean.

Sunset at the Ramparts

Enjoy golden-hour views along the fort walls, where ocean breezes and historic architecture create one of the most iconic sunset experiences in southern Sri Lanka.

Coastal Day Trips

Take short trips from Galle to nearby beaches and coastal viewpoints for swimming, relaxing, and experiencing the laid-back southern coastline atmosphere.

Plan Your Visit

Galle is ideal for travelers seeking heritage, coastal beauty, and a relaxed lifestyle. Stay in boutique hotels inside Galle Fort or beachfront resorts nearby for easy access to culture and ocean views.
