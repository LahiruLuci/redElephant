The Emerald Heart of Sri Lanka

Rise above the coastal heat into the refreshing embrace of Sri Lanka's Hill Country. This region is a breathtaking tapestry of emerald-green tea estates, cascading waterfalls, and mist-shrouded mountain peaks that reach towards the clouds.

Whether you're taking the iconic "most beautiful train ride in the world" to Ella, wandering through the colonial charm of Nuwara Eliya, or trekking the rugged landscape of Horton Plains, the highlands offer a serene escape into nature's purest form.
