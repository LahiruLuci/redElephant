Anuradhapura Sri Lanka

Where History Beckons and Spirits Soar

An Ancient Sacred Capital

Anuradhapura is located in Sri Lanka’s North Central Province and is one of the country’s most historically significant cities. Renowned as the first ancient capital of Sri Lanka, it is a vast sacred city surrounded by reservoirs, monastic complexes, and carefully planned ancient architecture.

As a UNESCO World Heritage Site, Anuradhapura holds immense religious and cultural importance. The city is home to the sacred Sri Maha Bodhi Tree, believed to be the oldest historically documented tree in the world, and numerous ancient stupas that reflect the advanced civilization and spiritual devotion of early Sri Lankan kingdoms.

Must Visit Attractions

01

Sri Maha Bodhi

A sacred Buddhist site and one of the most revered landmarks in Sri Lanka, believed to be the oldest historically documented tree in the world, worshipped for over two millennia.

02

Ruwanwelisaya

One of the most iconic stupas in Sri Lanka, known for its grand white dome and spiritual significance, reflecting the architectural brilliance of the ancient Anuradhapura Kingdom.

03

Jetavanaramaya

A monumental stupa and ancient monastic complex, celebrated as a masterpiece of early Sri Lankan engineering and one of the most significant structures in the sacred city.

04

Abhayagiri Monastery

A historic monastery complex featuring impressive ruins, ancient statues, and a stupa that once served as a major center of Buddhist learning and monastic life.

05

Kuttam Pokuna (Twin Ponds)

Two exquisitely carved ancient bathing pools, showcasing advanced hydraulic engineering and the refined craftsmanship of the Anuradhapura era.

Things To Do

Visit Sacred Buddhist Sites

Explore Anuradhapura’s most revered religious landmarks, including ancient stupas and sacred shrines, and witness daily rituals practiced by pilgrims from across the country.

Cycling Through Ancient Ruins

Cycle across the expansive sacred city to discover monastic complexes, reservoirs, and ancient architecture spread across one of South Asia’s largest archaeological sites.

Heritage Walks & Archaeological Tours

Join guided heritage walks to learn about the engineering, irrigation systems, and cultural achievements of Sri Lanka’s first ancient kingdom.

Plan Your Visit

Anuradhapura is ideal for travelers seeking spiritual depth and historical insight. Choose from heritage hotels and peaceful stays close to the sacred city and archaeological zones.
