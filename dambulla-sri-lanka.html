Dambulla Sri Lanka

Gateway to Cultural Splendor

  • Home
  • Culture Triangle
  • Dambulla

A Hub of History

Dambulla, located in the North Central Province, is a vibrant town that blends ancient history with modern connectivity. Recognized as a key transportation hub, it serves as the perfect gateway to Sri Lanka’s Cultural Triangle, offering easy access to Sigiriya, Polonnaruwa, and Anuradhapura.

At its heart lies the Golden Rock Cave Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage Site featuring five sanctuaries adorned with exquisite Buddhist murals and over 150 statues. From its towering granite outcrop, visitors are rewarded with breathtaking panoramic views, making Dambulla a must-visit for culture and nature lovers alike.

Must Visit Attractions

01

Dambulla Cave Temple

The best-preserved cave temple complex in Sri Lanka, boasting ancient Buddhist murals covering an area of 2,100 square meters and numerous Buddha statues.

02

Habarana

A central hub known for its close proximity to Minneriya and Kaudulla National Parks, famous for exciting elephant safaris and lush jungle landscapes.

03

Knuckles Mountain Range

A UNESCO World Heritage site offering rugged peaks, mist-shrouded valleys, and some of the most scenic trekking trails in the country.

04

Aluvihare Temple

A historic rock temple where the Tripitaka (Buddhist scriptures) was first written down on palm leaves, set amidst picturesque caves.

05

Spice Gardens

Discover Sri Lanka's world-famous spices like cinnamon, cardamom, and pepper in the lush gardens of Matale, located just south of Dambulla.

EXPERIENCE

Things To Do

Explore the Royal Caves

Climb the 160-meter rock to explore the five magnificent caves, each filled with vibrant paintings and statues dating back to the 1st century BC.

Hot Air Ballooning

Experience the Cultural Triangle from above with a sunrise hot air balloon ride, offering stunning views of Sigiriya and Dambulla’s landscapes.

Village & Jungle Tours

Take a bullock cart ride or a catamaran trip through the village lakes in Habarana to experience authentic rural Sri Lankan life.

Plan Your Visit

Dambulla offers a perfect mix of culture and nature. Stay in eco-friendly resorts like Heritance Kandalama or boutique hotels near the lake.
