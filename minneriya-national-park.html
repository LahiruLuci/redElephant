The Elephant Gathering

Nestled in the heart of Sri Lanka's North Central Province, Minneriya National Park stands as a testament to the country's rich biodiversity. Spanning over 8,800 hectares, this expansive sanctuary offers visitors an unparalleled opportunity to witness a diverse array of wildlife against the backdrop of picturesque landscapes.

The park is world-famous for "The Gathering," a natural phenomenon occurring annually between July and October, where hundreds of Asian elephants congregate around the Minneriya Tank in search of water and fresh grass‚Äîexposing visitors to one of the greatest wildlife spectacles on Earth.

Park Highlights

01

The Gathering

Witness hundreds of elephants converging on the Minneriya Tank during the dry season, a truly unique and awe-inspiring spectacle.

02

Minneriya Tank

The lifeblood of the park, this ancient reservoir built by King Mahasen supports a thriving ecosystem of aquatic life and birds.

03

Diverse Wildlife

Beyond elephants, spot Sambar deer, sloth bears, leopards, and the endemic Purple-faced Langur in the park's forests.

04

Avian Wonders

Home to a myriad of bird species including painted storks, great white pelicans, and gray herons fishing in the shallows.
