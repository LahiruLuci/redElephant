Things To Do Across Sri Lanka’s Cultural Heritage

Ancient history, sacred traditions, and unforgettable local experiences await you

Temple & Spiritual Tours
Visit sacred temples such as the Temple of the Tooth in Kandy and experience peaceful Buddhist rituals and blessings.
Kandy Esala Perahera Festival
Witness Sri Lanka’s grand cultural procession featuring decorated elephants, dancers, drummers, and sacred relics in Kandy.
Traditional Dance & Cultural Shows
Enjoy vibrant Kandyan dance performances, drumming, and cultural storytelling that bring Sri Lanka’s traditions to life.
Explore Ancient Cities
Walk through UNESCO heritage sites like Anuradhapura, Polonnaruwa, and Sigiriya, discovering Sri Lanka’s royal past and ancient architecture.
Sri Lankan Cooking Experience
Learn to prepare authentic Sri Lankan dishes using fresh local ingredients and traditional cooking methods.
