Plan Your Dream Honeymoon Journey Across Sri Lanka

Day 1
COASTAL POINT / AREA 01

Negombo Beach

Arrive in Sri Lanka and begin your honeymoon journey along the golden shores of Negombo, a charming coastal town just minutes from the international airport. Unwind after your flight with ocean views, gentle sea breezes, and a relaxed beachside atmosphere.

Attraction

Sunset Beach Walk, Dutch Canal

Best Buy

Romantic beach dinner, visit to a traditional fishing village

Day 2
COASTAL POINT / AREA 02

Kalpitiya Peninsula

ATravel north to Kalpitiya, one of Sri Lanka’s most exciting marine destinations. Famous for its rich marine biodiversity and steady winds, this area offers thrilling ocean experiences blended with untouched natural beauty.

Attraction

Bar Reef Marine Sanctuary

Best Buy

Snorkeling & scuba diving, dolphin and whale watching, kite surfing

Day 3
CULTURAL POINT / AREA 03

Sacred City of Kandy

Leave the coast and journey into the hill country to Kandy, a UNESCO World Heritage city surrounded by lush mountains. Discover Sri Lanka’s spiritual and cultural essence in a romantic and serene setting.

Attraction

Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic

Best Buy

Cultural dance performance, visit to Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage

Day 4
COASTAL POINT / AREA 04

Galle Fort & Southern Coast

Head south to Galle, where colonial elegance meets the Indian Ocean. Walk through cobblestone streets inside the historic Galle Fort and relax on nearby pristine beaches.

Attraction

Galle Fort (UNESCO World Heritage Site)

Best Buy

Beach relaxation, water sports, private candle-lit dinner by the sea

Day 5
COASTAL POINT / AREA 05

Bentota Beach

Bentota is a tropical paradise known for calm beaches and scenic waterways. A perfect destination for honeymooners seeking relaxation blended with soft adventure.

Attraction

Madu River Wetlands

Best Buy

River boat safari, turtle hatchery visit, luxury beach resort stay

Day 6
CITY POINT / AREA 06

Colombo City

Experience the modern side of Sri Lanka in Colombo, where colonial history blends seamlessly with luxury hotels, vibrant markets, and stylish cafés.

Attraction

Galle Face Green, Independence Square

Best Buy

City tour, shopping, fine dining experience

Day 7
FINAL POINT

Colombo → Airport

Enjoy a relaxed morning before your transfer to the airport, taking with you unforgettable memories of Sri Lanka’s beaches, culture, and romance.
