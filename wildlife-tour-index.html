Safari Experiences

Wildlife Encounters & Activities

From jeep safaris to boat rides, experience the best of Sri Lanka's fauna

Leopard Tracking
Join expert trackers in Yala and Wilpattu to spot the elusive Sri Lankan Leopard in the wild.
The Great Elephant Gathering
Witness the largest gathering of Asian elephants in the world at Minneriya and Kaudulla National Parks.
Sloth Bear Watching
Visit Wilpattu National Park during the Palu season to see Sloth Bears feeding on fruit.
Bird Watching
Explore Kumana and Bundala, havens for migratory birds, waterfowl, and flamingos.
Boat Safari
Experience a unique boat safari in Gal Oya National Park to see swimming elephants.
