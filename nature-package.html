Immerse yourself in lush rainforests, mist-covered mountains, and thrilling safaris on an unforgettable journey through Sri Lanka's wild heart.

Ayubowan & Welcome to the Wild!

Experience the untamed beauty of Sri Lanka with our curated nature and wildlife tour. Traverse through dense jungles, spot leopards and elephants in their natural habitats, and breathe in the fresh air of the central highlands.

From the biodiversity of Sinharaja Rainforest to the elephant gatherings at Minneriya, the leopards of Yala, and the scenic trails of Ella, this journey is perfect for nature lovers, photographers, and adventure seekers alike.

Discover the Natural Wonders of Sri Lanka

Embark on a breathtaking journey that takes you deep into the heart of nature. This itinerary connects pristine rainforests, majestic national parks, and serene highlands, offering a perfect blend of adventure, relaxation, and wildlife encounters.

Day 1
COASTAL POINT / AREA 01

Negombo

Arrive in Sri Lanka and relax in the coastal town of Negombo. Enjoy a boat ride in the Muthurajawela marsh, a bird watcher's paradise, to start your nature tour.

Attraction

Muthurajawela Wetlands, Negombo Lagoon

Activity

Boat Safari, Bird Watching

Day 2
RAINFOREST / AREA 02

Sinharaja Forest

Travel to Sinharaja, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and the country's last viable area of primary tropical rainforest. More than 60% of the trees are endemic and many of them are considered rare.

Attraction

Sinharaja Rain Forest Reserve, Waterfalls

Activity

Jungle Trekking, Bird Watching, Nature Photography

Day 3
WILDLIFE PARK / AREA 03

Udawalawe

Udawalawe National Park is famous for its large population of wild Asian elephants. The park's landscape is a mixture of abandoned teak plantations, grassland, and scrub jungle.

Attraction

Udawalawe National Park, Elephant Transit Home

Activity

Jeep Safari, Visiting Baby Elephants

Day 4
WILDLIFE PARK / AREA 04

Yala National Park

Move on to Yala, Sri Lanka's most popular national park. It has one of the highest densities of leopards in the world. Keep your eyes peeled for sloth bears, crocodiles, and diverse birdlife.

Attraction

Yala National Park, Sithulpawwa Rock Temple

Activity

Morning/Evening Jeep Safari, Wildlife Photography

Day 5
HILL COUNTRY / AREA 05

Ella

Head to the cool climates of Ella. This small town in the Badulla District is famous for its breathtaking views, hiking trails, and the iconic Nine Arch Bridge.

Attraction

Nine Arch Bridge, Little Adam's Peak, Ella Rock

Activity

Hiking, Scenic Train Watching, Relaxation

Day 6
HILL COUNTRY / AREA 06

Nuwara Eliya

Known as "Little England," Nuwara Eliya is surrounded by tea plantations and misty mountains. Visit Horton Plains National Park for a trek to World's End, a sheer precipice with a drop of about 4,000 feet.

Attraction

Horton Plains National Park, World's End, Baker's Falls

Activity

Trekking, Nature Walks, Tea Factory Visit

Day 7
NATURE & CULTURE / AREA 07

Kandy & Udawattakele

Visit Kandy, the hill capital. Explore Udawattakele Sanctuary, a historic forest reserve on a hill-ridge in the city. It is famous for its extensive avifauna.

Attraction

Udawattakele Sanctuary, Royal Botanical Gardens

Activity

Forest Walk, Orchid Garden Visit

Day 8
NATURE & HERITAGE / AREA 08

Sigiriya & Minneriya

Climb the Sigiriya Rock Fortress in the morning. In the afternoon, take a jeep safari in Minneriya National Park to witness the "Gathering" of wild elephants (seasonal).

Attraction

Sigiriya Lion Rock, Minneriya National Park

Activity

Rock Climbing, Elephant Safari

Final Point
FINAL POINT

Colombo → Airport

Enjoy a final glimpse of the island before your transfer to the airport, taking with you memories of Sri Lanka's incredible biodiversity and natural beauty.
