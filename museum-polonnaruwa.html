Meseum Polonnaruwa

Immerse yourself in the rich tapestry of Polonnaruwa's history at the Polonnaruwa Museum, where ancient artifacts and archaeological wonders come to life. Step into a world of discovery as you explore meticulously curated exhibits that showcase the cultural heritage and vibrant legacy of this UNESCO World Heritage Site.

From intricately carved statues to ancient relics unearthed from the ruins of Polonnaruwa, each artifact tells a compelling story of the city's illustrious past. Gain insights into the daily lives, customs, and beliefs of the ancient inhabitants as you wander through the museum's halls, guided by the expertise of knowledgeable curators.

Whether you're a history enthusiast or a casual visitor, the Polonnaruwa Museum offers a captivating journey through time, where every artifact serves as a window into the fascinating world of one of Sri Lanka's most iconic historical sites. Discover the secrets of Polonnaruwa's ancient civilization and uncover the mysteries that continue to intrigue scholars and travelers alike at the Polonnaruwa Museum.
