RIVER / AREA 02

Kitulgala (White Water Rafting)

Head to Kitulgala, the adventure capital. Tackle 5 major rapids and 4 minor rapids on the Kelani River.

Attraction

Kelani River

Activity

White Water Rafting, Canyoning

Day 3
HIGHLANDS / AREA 03

Adam's Peak / Nuwara Eliya

Depending on the season (Dec-May), climb Adam's Peak for sunrise. Or trek in Horton Plains National Park.

Attraction

Adam's Peak / Horton Plains

Activity

Hiking, Trekking

Day 4
HILL COUNTRY / AREA 04

Ella

Hike Ella Rock in the morning. In the afternoon, try the Flying Ravana Mega Zipline.

Attraction

Ella Rock, Nine Arch Bridge

Activity

Hiking, Ziplining

Day 5-6
EAST COAST / AREA 05

Arugam Bay (Seasonal) or South Coast

Head to the coast for surfing. Arugam Bay is best from April to October. Otherwise, head south to Mirissa/Weligama.

Attraction

Surfing Points

Activity

Surfing Lessons / Pro Surfing

Day 7
SOUTH / AREA 06

Hikkaduwa / Bentota

Enjoy water sports like jet skiing, windsurfing, or snorkeling in the Hikkaduwa coral sanctuary.

Attraction

Coral Reef, Bentota River

Activity

Snorkeling, Water Sports
