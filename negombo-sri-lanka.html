Where History Meets the Sea, and Every Moment Becomes a Memory

A Vibrant Coastal Paradise

Negombo is situated on the western coast of Sri Lanka, approximately 35 kilometers north of the capital city, Colombo. Conveniently located just a short drive from Bandaranaike International Airport, it serves as the perfect gateway for travelers exploring this enchanting island.

With a rich history spanning centuries, Negombo has been a prominent trading port since ancient times. The city's multicultural heritage beautifully blends Portuguese, Dutch, and British colonial influences with traditional Sri Lankan charm.

Must Visit Attractions

01

Negombo Beach

Golden sands stretching for kilometers along the coastline, offering the perfect setting for sunbathing, swimming, and water sports. Palm-lined shores dotted with vibrant fishing boats create a postcard-perfect paradise.

02

Dutch Fort

Built in the 17th century, this historic landmark reflects Negombo's rich colonial heritage. Remnants of original walls and gateways stand as testament to the city's fascinating past.

03

St. Mary's Church

Known as the "Queen of the Fishermen's Chapels," this striking Catholic church features impressive architecture and intricate stained glass windows, making it a beloved pilgrimage site.

04

Negombo Lagoon

A haven for birdwatchers and nature enthusiasts, home to diverse bird species including migratory visitors. Explore by boat or enjoy birdwatching from scenic observation points along the shore.

05

Fish Market

Experience the vibrant atmosphere of one of Sri Lanka's largest fishing ports. Watch fishermen haul in their daily catch and vendors showcase an impressive variety of fresh seafood.
